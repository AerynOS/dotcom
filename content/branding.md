## Names

**Aeryn Engineering** is the owner of this project along with it's associated tooling and infrastructure. The project should be referred to as "AerynOS", it should not be shortened to AOS or be referred to as "Aeryn" or "Aeryn OS" or amalgamated with other words like Operating System or Linux.
